About MTRP

The Mathematics Talent Reward Program (MTRP) is a prestigious mathematics competition designed to identify and nurture young mathematical talent across India. Conducted by the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, this program challenges students with elegant and thought-provoking mathematical problems.

A platform to showcase intellectual brilliance for high school students, coupled with attractive cash incentive and an opportunity to attend an exciting camp at the campus, MTRP is easily one of the most significant pan India math competitions at the high school level. It celebrates the brightest young minds across three age categories. If you derive pleasure from solving puzzles, this event is for you!
